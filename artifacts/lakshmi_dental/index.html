Meet Your Doctor

Meet Dr. Arvind P V

Dental Surgeon General Dentistry Cosmetic Dentistry Experienced Dental Care

Dr. Arvind P V is an experienced Dental Surgeon dedicated to providing high-quality, ethical, and patient-friendly dental care. At Lakshmi Dental Care, he focuses on delivering comfortable treatments using modern techniques and a personalized approach for every patient.

From routine dental checkups and teeth cleaning to root canal treatments, cosmetic dentistry, and smile restoration, Dr. Arvind P V believes in helping patients achieve long-term oral health with confidence and comfort.

Known for his calm approach and clear explanation of treatments, he has earned the trust of patients across Bengaluru and surrounding areas.
